# Data Processing Agreement (Draft)

> Draft template, Article 28 UK GDPR. Not legally binding until
> executed by both parties. Replace bracketed placeholders before
> signing.

**This Agreement** is entered into between:

- **[School name]** ("the Controller"), and
- **[The Makaton operator]** ("the Processor").

## 1. Subject matter and duration

The Processor processes pupil personal data on behalf of the
Controller for the duration of the pilot (or any subsequent
subscription term) and for 90 days after termination, after which all
pupil personal data shall be deleted or returned.

## 2. Nature and purpose of processing

Hosting, storing, and processing pupil identifiers, EHCP category
tags, selection events, session metadata, and prediction logs to
provide an in-classroom augmentative communication tool.

## 3. Types of personal data

- Pupil display name, year group, Makaton stage, depth setting,
  home language, EHCP category tags.
- Card-selection events with timestamps and dwell time.
- Session start/end and aggregate counters.

## 4. Categories of data subjects

- Pupils on the SEN register at the Controller's school.
- Teaching assistants and SENCos employed by the Controller.

## 5. Obligations of the Processor

The Processor shall:

a. process personal data only on documented instructions from the
   Controller, including transfers to a third country, unless required
   by UK law;
b. ensure persons authorised to process the personal data are under
   confidentiality obligations;
c. take all measures required pursuant to Article 32 (security);
d. respect the conditions in Article 28(2) and (4) for engaging
   sub-processors — current list in README;
e. assist the Controller with data-subject rights requests and
   provide a self-serve SAR + erasure interface;
f. assist the Controller in ensuring compliance with Arts. 32–36;
g. delete or return all personal data after end of services;
h. make available all information necessary to demonstrate compliance.

## 6. Sub-processors

Listed in README. The Processor will give 30 days' notice of any
change. The Controller may object.

## 7. International transfers

No transfers outside the UK or EEA without an Article 46 transfer
mechanism (IDTA or UK Addendum to EU SCCs).

## 8. Security

See `docs/DPIA.md` §5 for the technical and organisational measures.

## 9. Breach notification

The Processor shall notify the Controller without undue delay and in
any case within **24 hours** of becoming aware of a personal data
breach.

## 10. Audit

The Controller may audit the Processor's compliance once per year on
30 days' notice, or more frequently following a breach.
